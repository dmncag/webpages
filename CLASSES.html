BALANCE CLASSES

ADVANCED CALISTHENICS

Advanced Calisthenics is a progression from our Calisthenics class. You can join this class only once you have passed our "Calisthenics Proficiency Test" (validated by our coaches) and demonstrate sufficient body control, form and strength. This class goes beyond the basics of bodyweight training and allows you to explore more technical and strength demanding movements, and positions to teach the body how to coordinate its strength and balance through the core and into the limbs. Talk to our coaches for more information on how you can join our Advanced Calisthenics class at TripleFit!

ENDURANCE CLASSES

ATHLEAN

A class combining endurance focused aerobic intervals and active recovery with high repetition, lighter weight sets of strength-endurance training. The goal of this class is to keep your heart rate elevated and your body blasting through the calories in every session.

BOOTCAMP

Bootcamp with TripleFit is a structured outdoor fitness and conditioning program designed to get you in great shape fast by training all components of fitness (cardiovascular endurance, flexibility, strength, balance, stability, core strength, speed, and agility) into one jam-packed session.

With each session never the same, our Bootcamp sessions are designed to challenge participants individually, allowing everyone to give 100%,regardless of age or current fitness levels to maximize effectiveness, fat burn, and fun! Our Bootcamp classes are based mainly around bodyweight exercises, in formats of circuits, intervals, games, races, and creative partner exercises. Our Bootcamp coaches are here to motivate and guide you to perform better in each and every class!

STRENGTH CLASSES

20/40/20

20/40/20 is a high-intensity interval training workout consisting of exercises done in a circuit - a combination of 20 sets of 40 seconds work followed by 20 seconds rest to improve your endurance and strength. This class is designed to get you into the best shape of your life, challenging but always modifiable for all levels.

HIIT

HIIT, or High-Intensity Interval Training, is the next level of training which keeps your body guessing and the heart pumping in a dynamic environment. Our class content is always changing, but it always begins with a movement based warm-up followed by a circuit consisting of a blend of bodyweight, weights, and cardio exercises. Every day is different so expect the unexpected and your body will keep adapting to the variety it gets and you'll be one step closer to your fitness goals!
